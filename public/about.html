Our Mission

Democratizing Embodied AI Research

We believe embodied AI should not be gatekept by expensive hardware, complex middleware, or closed ecosystems. 3we exists to remove three barriers: prohibitive costs, ROS2 complexity, and the sim-to-real gap.

accessibility_new

Accessibility

AI researchers shouldn't need to learn ROS2. Our Python API reduces 60 lines of boilerplate to 5, letting you focus on the science.

savings

Affordability

A research-capable robot for under $500. Complete open BOM with supplier links. No proprietary lock-in, no hidden costs.

repeat

Reproducibility

Same code runs in simulation and on hardware — guaranteed. Standardized benchmarks and public leaderboard ensure verifiable results.
