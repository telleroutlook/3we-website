Frequently Asked Questions

Can I mix components from different SKUs?

Absolutely. The BOM is modular — start with Basic and add LiDAR, camera, or AI acceleration later. The PBC-34 bus makes upgrades plug-and-play.

Where can I buy the components?

Our BOM includes direct links to AliExpress, Mouser, and DigiKey for every part. Most components are globally available with 1-2 week shipping.

Do you ship pre-assembled units internationally?

Pre-assembled units ship worldwide. Contact us for shipping estimates and bulk pricing for labs and educational institutions.

What software is included?

All SKUs use the same threewe Python SDK. The full software stack (firmware, ROS2 nodes, SDK, Gymnasium environments) is free and open-source regardless of hardware tier.

Is this suitable for outdoor use?

The Basic and Standard tiers are designed for indoor environments. The Industrial tier with IP54 protection supports light outdoor use (dust and splash resistant).
